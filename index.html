My Views

My Views

I've been attending Bluffdale City Council meetings for almost three years to learn more about the city's future growth and development. I see a lot of opportunities for us as a city to maintain a balanced Bluffdale that preserves the past, and grows gracefully into the future. I'll work hard to do the following:

  • Be available to discuss with the residents of Bluffdale what their vision for the city is and how I can best represent them.

  • Support retail zoning from 14600 S east of the bridge, and Porter Rockwell Boulevard to help grow our tax base.

  • Be fiscally responsible by providing for basic public services first, in support of the growth of the city.

  • Maintain current zoning for existing larger lots and agricultural areas, and find solutions to blend these existing areas responsibly with new growth.

  • Constantly learn from the City Staff and other council members about new and upcoming issues the city is facing and work together to find innovative solutions.

About Me

Ulises Flynn, a first-generation American, was born and raised in Caracas, Venezuela until 3rd grade when he moved to Billerica, MA. He served an LDS church mission to Rio De Janeiro, Brazil. He attended Brigham Young University where he completed a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. He has worked for Lockheed Martin, then as an Air Force civilian for Hill Air Force base in Utah for 6 years. Currently he works for a startup company in Lehi called Partner Fusion where he is a Senior Software Engineer.

Throughout his career, Ulises has volunteered in community STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) programs. At Hill Air Force base he served as a mentor for a work/study program with Park City students to help them work alongside the base’s bomb squad. He was also a judge in the Utah State Science Fair held at Weber State University, and at other STEM events held locally. Recently he was a mentor for the First Tech Challenge team “Chewbotccas” that went on to the state competitions in 2017.

While residing in Clinton, UT, Ulises was involved as a founding member for Voyage Academy charter school, and helped the school open in 2013. In 2015 Ulises and his family moved to Bluffdale where he to got involved in the community by attending City Council and Planning Committee meetings. He completed the Bluffdale CERT program, and is an assistant in the IND Section for the city’s Emergency Preparedness group. He is also HAM certified and has attended the city’s HAM group meetings.

Ulises is married to Kristal Flynn and they have four children (3 girls and 1 boy) ranging from 9 years old to a 1 year old. His daughter Isabel attended Bluffdale Elementary and is currently attending Summit Academy Independence Campus. Their children also attend MCD dance academy and Fuller’s Swim School in Bluffdale.
